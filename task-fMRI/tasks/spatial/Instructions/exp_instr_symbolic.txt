In this task, you will compare different spatial
arrangements of the same objects.

During each trial, you will first be presented with three
specific items in a specific spatial configuration.
Then, you will be shown three additional configurations
of the same three items.

Your task is to determine which of these corresponds
to a slight rotation of the original configuration.

To choose the leftmost option: press '2'.
To choose the center option: press '3'.
To choose the rightmost option: press '4'.


Press any button to continue...
