In this task, you will be comparing different
spatial arrangements of the same objects.

At the start each trial, three objects will
appear in a specific spatial configuration at
the top of the screen. Then, three additional
configurations of the same objects will appear
at the bottom.

Your task is to select the option at the bottom
that best matches the original layout.

To choose the leftmost option: press '2'.
To choose the center option: press '3'.
To choose the rightmost option: press '4'.


Press any button to continue...
