In this task, your memory of the paired items
that were previously shown to you will be tested.

At each trial, one item will be shown at the top of the
screen and three others will be displayed at the bottom.

Using the button box, your task is to select the item
that was initially paired up with the one at the top of
the screen.

To choose the leftmost option: press '2'
To choose the center option: press '3'
To choose the rightmost option: press '4'


Press any button to continue...
